About Access Tech

Trusted advisors who happen to own the equipment.

Access Tech provides fire management and land stewardship consulting — with in-house implementation — across North Idaho and Eastern Washington. We don't sell machine time. We help landowners assess wildfire risk, understand their property's condition, and manage their land for the long term.

Our work is guided by formal training in Forest Resource Management and Fire Ecology & Management, and ongoing professional work in the forestry industry. The goal is simple: give landowners effective, practical solutions that improve the health, usability, and safety of their property.

How We Work

Evaluate first. Prescribe second. Implement third.

Land stewardship is a process, not a one-time job. Every property starts with an assessment — the equipment comes later.

01

Assess

We walk your property and evaluate wildfire risk, fuels, forest health, and existing conditions.

02

Prescribe

You get practical, science-based treatment recommendations — grounded in forestry, not a sales pitch.

03

Prioritize

Projects are sequenced by wildfire risk, forest health, and your objectives and budget.

04

Implement

We carry out the treatments with our in-house equipment — or trusted partners for specialized work.

05

Maintain

We monitor results and recommend long-term maintenance cycles that keep your land protected.

Implementation

Services

When a treatment plan calls for equipment, we do the work ourselves — the full range of forestry and land-improvement services, in-house or with trusted partners.

Forestry Mulching

Grind brush, saplings, and overgrowth into nutrient-rich mulch in a single pass — no piles, no burning.

Fuel Reduction

Reduce the ladder fuels and dense vegetation that let wildfire climb and spread across your land.

Fire Mitigation

Create defensible space around homes and structures to give your property a fighting chance.

Property Cleanup

Clear years of overgrowth, deadfall, and debris to reclaim ground you can actually use.

Understory Thinning

Selectively thin crowded understory to improve forest health and let your best trees thrive.

Trail Creation

Open up walking, riding, and recreational trails that make your acreage enjoyable and accessible.

Access Improvements

Build and widen access routes so you, your equipment, and emergency services can get where you need to go.

Small Tree Removal

Remove unwanted small trees and saplings without tearing up the ground you want to keep.

Land Improvement

Turn neglected acreage into healthy, productive, maintainable land that works for how you live.
