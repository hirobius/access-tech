Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Access Tech ("we," "us") handles information collected through this website.

Information we collect

When you submit the estimate request form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, property address (optional), approximate acreage, project description, and any photo you choose to upload.

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your request, prepare an estimate, and contact you about your project. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

How it's handled

Form submissions are delivered to us by email through our form provider (Web3Forms). This website is served by a third-party hosting provider. These providers process the information only to deliver our service.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If we add analytics in the future, we will use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track you across other websites.

Data retention & your choices

We keep estimate requests only as long as needed to serve you. To ask what information we have, or to request that we delete it, contact us using the details below.
